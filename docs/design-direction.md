# Foodie Me Design Direction

## Chosen visual direction

Image #5 is the chosen header direction for this first web MVP.

Use it as the inspiration for the top visual: a cute, clean illustrated food truck / mini cafe cart with a yellow striped awning, roof plants, and a pastel cream / peach / mint / teal palette.

## Art direction guardrails

- Mobile-first and polished, not childish.
- Clear vector-background feeling.
- No fake Japanese, Korean, or unreadable decorative text.
- No busy background.
- No gacha mechanics or clutter.
- Food and couple-tracking content should stay readable and useful.

## Palette notes

- Cream base: warm off-white and butter tones
- Peach accents: soft coral / apricot cards
- Mint and teal: dashboard accents, plants, and status chips
- Dark warm brown: primary text for a softer food-journal feel

## Product UI notes

The dashboard reserves room for the core hierarchy and future AI review flow:

- city cards
- active food quests
- next places to try
- couple rankings and rating cards
- AI research review teaser
